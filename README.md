## Commands
- `Spawn the Evil Entity`: Opens the Evil Entity panel
- `Delete the Evil Entity`: Closes the Evil Entity panel

## Features
An evil, grinning emoji that follows your cursor, insults you, and screams at you if you move your cursor.
Very irritable I must say.

## Notes
- The entity tracks your mouse and reacts to movement
- It runs in a seperate panel of its own
- If it gets annoying, use the stop command

## How it helps devs
The Evil, by insulting you, coerces you work harder so that you can prove it wrong!
